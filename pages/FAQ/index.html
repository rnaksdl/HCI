Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KillerSale POS system for small businesses.

General Questions

What is KillerSale POS?

KillerSale is a free, all-in-one Point of Sale system specifically designed for small businesses. Our platform combines customer-facing and business management features in a single, intuitive application with a clean black and white interface focused on minimizing cognitive load and streamlining operations.

What makes KillerSale different from other POS systems?

Our system is built on human-computer interaction principles rather than traditional POS conventions. We've eliminated visual clutter, reduced unnecessary steps, and designed intuitive workflows that match how people naturally think. Plus, KillerSale is completely free for small businesses, unlike competitors who charge monthly subscription fees.

Is KillerSale really free? What's the catch?

Yes, KillerSale is completely free to use. We believe small businesses shouldn't have to pay hundreds or thousands of dollars for essential POS functionality. Our business model is based on optional premium features that will be introduced in the future, but all core functionality will remain free.

Setup & Implementation

How long does it take to set up KillerSale?

Most businesses can be up and running in under 30 minutes. We've designed the onboarding process to require minimal information upfront, with optional details that can be added later as needed.

Do I need special hardware to run KillerSale?

No! KillerSale runs in any standard web browser, so you can use your existing computers, tablets, or smartphones. For best results, we recommend devices with screens at least 7" in size.

Can I import my existing menu and business data?

Yes. KillerSale supports importing menu items and business information via CSV files. During setup, you'll have the option to upload your existing data or create your menu from scratch.

Features & Functionality

What analytics does KillerSale provide?

Our analytics dashboard provides three key visualizations:

  • Pie charts showing your most popular menu items
  • Line graphs tracking your profit trends over time
  • Bar charts displaying customer foot traffic patterns
How does the menu creation system work?

Our menu creation tool lets you:

  • Create and organize items by category
  • Add descriptions, prices, and optional images
  • Set up modification options (like "no onions" or "extra cheese")
  • Instantly publish changes to your customer-facing menu
How does KillerSale handle orders?

The order management system clearly separates current orders from past orders with status indicators that can be understood at a glance. Orders can be updated, modified, and tracked through preparation and delivery stages with minimal clicks.

Can customers place orders directly?

Yes! KillerSale provides a customer-facing menu interface that allows for self-service ordering. Customers can browse your menu, add items to their cart, make modifications, and pay through a streamlined checkout process.

Technical Requirements

What browsers does KillerSale support?

KillerSale works with all major modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (version 88 or newer).

Is KillerSale mobile-friendly?

Yes, our responsive design works on both desktop and mobile devices. The staff interface is optimized for tablets and larger screens, while the customer ordering interface is fully optimized for smartphones.

What happens if my internet goes down?

KillerSale includes offline functionality that allows basic operations to continue during temporary internet outages. Orders and payments are cached locally and synchronized automatically when your connection is restored.

Data & Security

How is my business data protected?

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure password storage with bcrypt hashing
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant data practices
Who owns the data in KillerSale?

You own your business data - period. We do not sell or share your information with third parties. We only use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our service.

Can I export my data from KillerSale?

Yes, you can export your business data, including sales history, menu items, and customer information at any time through your account settings.

What if I find a bug or have a feature request?

How often is KillerSale updated?

We release updates approximately once a month, focusing on performance improvements, bug fixes, and new features based on user feedback. Updates are automatic and require no action on your part.
